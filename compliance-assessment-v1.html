Who We Are

Our Mission

Led by a growing coalition of over 400 Hilltown residents, we strongly oppose aggressive, high-risk industrial solar and battery storage projects that threaten the character, safety, and sovereignty of our communities.

"People think if it's clean energy, it must be low risk. Nothing could be further from the truth. This is a disaster waiting to happen."

We do not oppose renewable energy. We oppose the industrial plundering of our sacred land — our farmland, our watersheds, and our right to determine our own future.

Battery Fire Risks

Thermal runaway fires release toxic chemicals no volunteer fire department is equipped to handle.

Land & Wildlife

Thousands of acres of prime farmland, forest, and wildlife habitat permanently destroyed.

Water Supplies

Industrial installations threaten the aquifers and streams that feed our wells and watersheds.

Self-Governance

State energy mandates override local zoning laws, stripping towns of the right to govern themselves.
